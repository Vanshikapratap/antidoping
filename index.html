Athlete Notification

The testing process begins when a Doping Control Officer (DCO) notifies the athlete of their selection for testing.

  • Athlete is notified in person by the DCO
  • Rights and responsibilities are explained
  • Athlete must sign the notification form
  • Athlete must remain in direct observation

Initial Documentation

The athlete provides necessary documentation and declares any medications or supplements.

Sample Collection

The athlete provides the required sample under direct observation of a same-gender DCO.

  • Selection of collection vessel
  • Minimum volume requirements explained
  • Direct observation protocol
  • Sample verification process

Sample Sealing

The sample is sealed in tamper-evident bottles using a unique coding system.

  • Verification of bottle numbers
  • Secure sealing process
  • Sample division (A and B samples)
  • Tamper-evident verification

Final Documentation

Complete the doping control form and verify all information.

  • Review all documented information
  • Verify sample codes
  • Sign final documentation
  • Receive athlete copy
